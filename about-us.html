About Us

HERMIT GARMENTS offers a curated collection of pre-owned garments that have been carefully sourced by its founder.

As many things, second-hand clothing is not a human-made invention. It can be traced back to hermit crabs.

These crustaceans have a distinct way of searching for abandoned sea snail shells, serving as their homes and clothes.

Similarly, I believe in offering new life to otherwise thrown-away garments, allowing them to continue their journey with the next HERMIT.
